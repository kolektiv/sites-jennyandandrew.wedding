Times, Dates, Places

When

1pm, Saturday 18th March, 2017 (if you're coming to the reception, it starts around 6pm — but it is a pub, and it's open earlier...)

Where

The Star Inn, Alfriston, East Sussex

Ceremony and Reception

Both the ceremony and reception will be taking place in the Star Inn, meaning there's no hassle of travelling around during the day. The ceremony and wedding breakfast are likely to continue until around 6pm, at which point the reception will begin — there will be food, and music, and it is a pub after all...

Don't worry about arriving too early — the pub will be open!

Accommodation

There are several options if you're planning on staying overnight. Staying at The Star Inn is probably the easiest — and if you say you're with the wedding party, you'll receive a discounted room rate.

Also in Alfriston is Deans Place Hotel which also offers excellent accommodation. There are other local options within walking distance.

If you're thinking of staying slightly further afield, both Brighton and Eastbourne are within practical driving distance and offer a huge variety of accommodation options.

Travel

Alfriston is not too difficult to get to. Driving directions to The Star Inn are straightforward and local traffic tends to be reasonable.

If you're planning on arriving by public transport, the nearest station is Berwick which has regular direct trains from Brighton (which in turn is easily accessible from London). Berwick Station is a little under three miles from The Star Inn, so while it is walk-able, we would suggest a taxi.

If you're coming from the surrounding area and would be interested in organised transport to and from the venue (perhaps picking up/dropping off in Brighton and Worthing) please email us to express an interest — if enough people would like to do so, we may be able to arrange transport.
